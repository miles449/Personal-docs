## Signature Bowls

All signatures come in Mini, Regular, or Large. Proteins can be swapped on request.

### 🌊 The Kaimana
**Base:** Brown rice · **Protein:** Ahi tuna · **Toppings:** Mango salsa, edamame, cucumber, crispy shallots, nori · **Sauce:** Original shoyu glaze

### 🏄 The Pipeline
**Base:** White rice · **Protein:** Salmon · **Toppings:** Avocado, pickled red onion, masago, sesame seeds, green onion · **Sauce:** Spicy mayo

### 🌺 The Hanalei
**Base:** Mixed greens · **Protein:** Yellowtail · **Toppings:** Pineapple, shredded cabbage, jalapeño, macadamia nuts, micro cilantro · **Sauce:** Citrus ponzu

### 🌿 The Makaha *(Vegan)*
**Base:** Cauliflower rice · **Protein:** Marinated tofu · **Toppings:** Shredded kale, beet, edamame, radish, hemp seeds · **Sauce:** Ginger miso

### 🔥 The Sunset Beach
**Base:** White rice · **Protein:** Spicy tuna · **Toppings:** Jalapeño, kimchi, cucumber, crispy shallots, nori · **Sauce:** Double spicy mayo
