We source from a small, trusted network of suppliers. Most of our fish is processed and delivered within 48 hours of catch. At least 40% of our produce comes from farms within 150 miles of each location.

## Proteins

| Protein | Source | Style |
|---------|--------|-------|
| Ahi Tuna | Pacific Seafood Co. (Hawaii) — MSC certified, pole & line caught | Raw |
| Salmon | Sitka Salmon Shares (Alaska) — MSC certified, wild-caught | Raw or seared |
| Yellowtail | Santa Barbara Fish Market | Raw |
| Shrimp | Gulf Wild (Gulf of Mexico) — wild-caught, GPS-tracked vessels | Tempura |
| Octopus (Tako) | Anova Food (Portugal/Spain) — slow-braised, then seared | Cooked |
| Teriyaki Chicken | Mary's Free Range (Fresno, CA) | Cooked |
| Marinated Tofu | Hodo Foods (Oakland, CA) | Vegan |

<Warning>
  Consuming raw or undercooked fish may increase risk of foodborne illness. Not recommended for pregnant individuals, elderly guests, young children, or those with compromised immune systems.
</Warning>
