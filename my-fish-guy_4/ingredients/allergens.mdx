<Warning>
  Our kitchens handle **fish, shellfish, soy, sesame, eggs, wheat, and tree nuts** daily. We cannot guarantee a fully allergen-free environment. Please inform your server of any allergies when ordering.
</Warning>

## Major Allergens Present

| Allergen | Found In |
|----------|---------|
| **Fish** | All raw and seared fish proteins |
| **Shellfish** | Shrimp tempura, masago, octopus |
| **Gluten/Wheat** | Wonton crisps, shrimp tempura batter |
| **Eggs** | Spicy mayo, sriracha aioli, yuzu vinaigrette, tempura batter |
| **Soy** | All soy-based sauces, tofu, edamame, miso |
| **Sesame** | Sesame oil (multiple sauces), sesame seeds, furikake |
| **Tree Nuts** | Macadamia nuts |

## Gluten-Free Sauces
Original shoyu glaze (tamari-based) · Spicy mayo · Citrus ponzu · Ginger miso · Sriracha aioli · Yuzu vinaigrette

  For a complete ingredient list for any item, email us at [allergens@myfishguy.com](mailto:allergens@myfishguy.com) or ask a team member in-store.
</Info>
