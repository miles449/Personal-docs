We're not a perfect company. We use plastic lids. Our supply chain generates more carbon than we'd like. We'd rather be honest about the gaps than paper over them.

## What We're Doing Well

- **Sustainable seafood** — All fish meets or exceeds MSC certification. Supplier list published quarterly.
- **Local produce** — At least 40% sourced within 150 miles of each location, tracked per city per quarter.
- **Food waste** — Small-batch prep model. End-of-day surplus goes to local food pantries (11 partner orgs) and composting. **94% of food waste diverted from landfill in 2024.**
- **Fair labor** — Living wage in every market. Healthcare for 30+ hour employees. Profit-sharing for all staff.

## What We're Working On

- **Packaging** — Compostable bowls and bamboo utensils, but lids are still plastic. Expecting a fix by end of 2025.
- **Carbon footprint** — Full scope 3 audit underway with Carbon Lighthouse. Targets to be set by Q1 2026.
- **Delivery packaging** — Third-party delivery platforms control their own packaging. We're advocating for better standards.
